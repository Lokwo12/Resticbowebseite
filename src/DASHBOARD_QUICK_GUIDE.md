## 🎯 **Common Workflows**

### **Adding a New Program**
1. Click "Programs" in sidebar
2. Click "Add Program" button
3. Fill in title, description, content
4. Select category
5. Upload image
6. Click "Create"

### **Managing Contact Messages**
1. Click "Contacts" in sidebar
2. Filter by status if needed
3. Click "View" to see details
4. Type reply in dialog
5. Click "Send Reply"
6. Status auto-updates to "Responded"

### **Approving Volunteers**
1. Click "Volunteers" in sidebar
2. Filter by "Pending" status
3. Review applicant details
4. Click "Approve" or "Reject"
5. Status updates automatically

### **Managing Users** (Super Admin)
1. Click "Users" in sidebar
2. Use filters to find users
3. Click "Edit" to modify
4. Change role or status
5. Click "Update User"

### **Bulk Deleting Items**
1. Select checkboxes on items
2. Bulk action toolbar appears
3. Click "Delete Selected"
4. Confirm deletion
5. Items removed
